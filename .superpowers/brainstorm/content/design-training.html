Section 3: Model Training & Validation

30-Day Forward Forecasting Strategy

Forecasting Horizon: 30 Trading Days

The model is now configured to predict the price target exactly 30 trading days into the future. This longer horizon captures medium-term trends rather than short-term noise.

Pros

  • Captures multi-week trend momentum
  • Less susceptible to daily volatility
  • Aligned with monthly investment cycles

Cons

  • Greater uncertainty (wider confidence bands)
  • Requires longer look-ahead for training labels

Training Strategy

Walk-Forward Validation

Train on data up to Year X, validate on Year X+1. This prevents "look-ahead bias" where the model accidentally learns from future data.

Early Stopping

Monitor validation loss (Pinball Loss). Stop training when the model stops improving on unseen data to prevent overfitting.

Model Persistence

Models will be saved as .pt (PyTorch) files. The MLModelService will handle automatic reloading of the weights during the Inference phase of the pipeline.
