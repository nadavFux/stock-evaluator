Section 2: Data Pipeline & Feature Engineering

Transforming raw market data into a 30-day sequence for the LSTM

The 12-Feature Vector

Each day in our 30-day sequence will consist of the following features, normalized to a [0, 1] or [-1, 1] range:

1. MA Gap
Price / 200-day MA
2. MA Dist
abs(1 - MA Gap)
3. Rating
Analyst Score (1-5)
4. Momentum
MA Trend (slope)
5. RVOL
Volume / 30-day Avg
6. PEG Ratio
PE / Growth Rate
7. RSI (14)
Rel. Strength Index
8. ATR %
Volatility / Price
9. MACD Hist
Trend Convergence
10. BB %B
Bollinger Position
11. Volatility
20-day Std Dev
12. Sector RS
Rel. Sector Strength

Preprocessing Workflow

1 Indicators: Calculate all 12 technicals for every day in the stock's history.
2 Scaling: Apply StandardScaler (z-score) to ensure LSTM stability.
3 Windowing: Group consecutive 30-day blocks into tensors: (Batch, 30, 12).
4 Labelling: Target is the stock price in 5 trading days.
